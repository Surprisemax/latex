\section*{Introduction} 
LaTeX is a widely used typesetting system for technical and scientific documentation \cite{ref1}.It is            
especially popular in mathematics and computer science because of its powerful handling of formulas \cite{ref2}. 
Researchers prefer LaTeX for writing research papers and theses \cite{ref3}. 
It provides automatic numbering of sections, equations, and references \cite{ref4}. Many academic journals require 
submissions in LaTeX format \cite{ref5}.      
%\cite used to insert citations 
LaTeX also supports bibliography management and citation formatting \cite{ref6}. 
It ensures consistency and professional formatting in documents \cite{ref7}. 
Cross-referencing features make it easy to manage large documents \cite{ref8}. 
Packages allow customization and additional functionalities \cite{ref9}. 
Therefore, LaTeX is an essential tool for academic writing \cite{ref10}.
